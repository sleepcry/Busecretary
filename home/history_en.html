Historical request/report are all listed here by time order. Relative requests and reports are put together.
You can expand a request and then view the detail of the relative reports.

Operation on historical request/report

  • delete a request/report. Warning: this operation will delete all relative report/request.
  • resend a request/report.
  • forward a report.
  • answer a request.
  • view a report on map.
  • decline a request.

The history will not refresh automatically if you received a new request/report while you are process the history.
You need to do it manually by menu -> refresh
. You can change the view mode by menu -> filter.
