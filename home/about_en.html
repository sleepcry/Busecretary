What is Weiz?

Weiz is a free service which is composed with a web and an app running on Android with the purpose that making
sharing location as easy as plain text.

The app is not essential to use the service, as an alterative way, you can use the service by accessing Weiz Web ,
but we strongly recommand you Download and install the app on you mobile phone to share and manage you location
more effectively

Rquirement to use Weiz

Everyone having a phone to send SMS can use the Weiz service. To use all feature of Weiz, you need the
following infrastructures:
  • GPS: to get your current location.
  • Internet: to make your location more accurate.
  • Android 2.2+, to use the Weiz app.

How to use the Weiz

  • request location from a person whose location you want to know.
  • respond to request from someone you'd like to share location with.
  • report location to someone requesting your location or share location forwardly.
  • manage history of your requesting and reporting
